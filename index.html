Russian Troll Tweets and Their Role in the 2016 Elections

ADA Project

December 2018

Jean-Baptiste Prost, Nicolas Gandar, & Antoine Spahr

#Abstract

The outcome of the 2016 US election were a surprise, only few experts predicted a Trump win. Since then, it has been revealed that the Russian might have had an influence on the election through their action on social media, especially Twitter. It has been revealed that many Twitter acounts were actually troll accounts trying to play with the public opinion. The focus is given to accounts characterized as Right or Left: which depends on the content they have published.
